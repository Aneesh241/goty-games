GOTY Winners from 2015 to 2023

Well... Each year a game is selected as the best game out of all the games which has released that year. Here are the list of the games that won the GOTY title from 2015 to 2023.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Year: 2015

Genre: Action RPG, Open World, Fantasy

Developer: CD Projekt Red

Step into the shoes of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter searching for his adopted daughter in a war-torn world filled with mythical creatures, political intrigue, and dark magic.

Overwatch

Year: 2016

Genre: FPS, Multiplayer

Developer: Blizzard Entertainment

Overwatch introduced the world to a team-based hero shooter with a diverse cast of characters, each bringing unique abilities and playstyles to fast-paced, objective-based battles.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Year: 2017

Genre: Action-Adventure, Open World

Developer: Nintendo EPD

Link awakens in a ruined kingdom and must explore a vast open world, uncovering secrets and regaining his strength to defeat the evil Calamity Ganon.

God of War

Year: 2018

Genre: Action, Adventure, Mythology

Developer: Santa Monica Studio

Kratos, now a father, embarks on a deeply personal journey with his son, Atreus, in a world inspired by Norse mythology.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Year: 2019

Genre: Action, Adventure, Souls-like

Developer: FromSoftware

Play as a shinobi warrior on a quest to rescue his kidnapped lord in a brutal, fast-paced action game that tests reflexes and precision.

The Last of Us Part II

Year: 2020

Genre: Action-Adventure, Survival

Developer: Naughty Dog

Ellie embarks on a revenge-driven journey, confronting the moral consequences of her actions in a hauntingly beautiful post-apocalyptic world.

It Takes Two

Year: 2021

Genre: Action, Adventure, Puzzle, Co-op

Developer: Hazelight Studios

A couple on the brink of divorce is transformed into dolls and must navigate whimsical, ever-changing levels to find their way back to their daughter.

Elden Ring

Year: 2022

Genre: Action RPG, Open World

Developer: FromSoftware

Set in a massive, lore-rich world, players must explore, battle formidable foes, and restore the Elden Ring.

Baldur's Gate 3

Year: 2023

Genre: RPG, Strategy

Developer: Larian Studios

In this Dungeons & Dragons-inspired RPG, players make impactful choices that shape the fate of their party and the world.
